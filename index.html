Philosophy

Money is one of the most important things of life. Money is limited. Thus we must manage it well. But it can be boring. Writing down every dime we spend is very mainstream. How about click a photo? How about scan the receipt?

SpendJoy allows you to record your expenses with image processing and optical character recognition which minimizes the need of typing. Along with that SpendJoy gives your report generations that you need to understand the details of your expenses. It also tells you, how much you have spent and what is your present situation in terms of your budget.

Manually Expense Record

Spendjoy allows you to manually keep tabs on your expenses by using manual inputs to update the database.Spendjoy gives you a before storyline section where you can see your expenses with all transection details and images

Image Processing and Optical Character Recognition

Spendjoy gives you options to click photos of the objects that you spend your money on and the app will suggest you what it is by processing the image.

Spendjoy also allows you to record bills and receipts by using optical charecter Recognition.Just click image, Scan receipt and acknowledge the summary to store the expenses in your storyline

What is this app all about

Spendjoy is an expense tracking mobile application that uses image processing and optical character recognition to record expenses and produce enhanced charts and reports

  • Click images of your expenses and bills to store
  • Get alerts on budgets and expenditures
  • Manage your debts with alarms and reminders.
